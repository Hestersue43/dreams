About Us

Dreams of Millions, Inc. was formed as a 501(c) 3 non-profit social enterprise for those who:

 

 

  • lack community influence
  • are under-resourced
  • need increased skills
  • require unique training and development
  • have irregular employmnet
  • have additional needs that affects their lives
