About Us  

Dreams of Millions, Inc. was formed as a 501(c) 3 non-profit social enterprise for those who:

  • lack community influence
  • are under-resourced
  • need increased skills
  • require unique training and development
  • have irregular employment
  • have additional needs that affect their lives

Meet the Ceo  

Desiree' Williams, MBA, is creative and passionate about helping people grow to new levels both personally and professionally. She is the Co-founder and CEO of Dreams of Millions, Inc., providing training and leadership development opportunities for under-resourced participants.
