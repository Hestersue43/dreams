Background

Dreams of Millions, Incorporated

Since April 2011, Dreams of Millions, Incorporated has been fulfilling its mission by helping people in the community to work toward employment and independence. We focus on developing participants for potential employment.

We partner with various programs in the area, such as Greene County Ohio Means, CareSource, Opportunities for Ohioans with Disabilities, Reentry and Second Chance, Goodwill Easter Seals of Greater Dayton, and TCN Behavioral Health, and the Greene County Prosecutor’s Office.

Our View On Employability Skills
Employability skills are the basic skills, attitudes and behaviors that are needed to find and keep employment. Employability skills can be put in to three categories:
  • Basic skills: the ability to communicate, think and learn.
  • Interpersonal skills: positive attitudes and behaviors, responsibility and adaptability.
  • Teamwork skills: the ability to work with others on a job and achieve results.
