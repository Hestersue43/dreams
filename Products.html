Our organization works like a volunteer inernship program. Women gift us with their time, an average of 24 hours monthly, or about 6 hours weekly.

The team of women manage the logistics of receiving , processing, and filling orders, contstructing the product, managing the warehousing of the products and supplies, maintaining inventory, shipping the product, assisting with quality improvement, and assisting with marketing management. They also mentor new team members.

Strengths Based Approaches to Improve Confidence
  • We work with clients to design their personalized portfolio to help them stand out from the crowd.
  • We educate clients on how to recognize their strengths and emphasize them in their career portfolio.
  • We help participants learn where their weaknesses are and how to compensate for them.
  • We support team members taking their portfolio to their interview to show a potential employer or manager samples of their work: certificates, service the community, etc.
  • We show patrons why an employer needs them or why they should be promoted, and how to incorporate their experiences to promote their strengths.
